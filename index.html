KEY FEATURES

For those who are not up to date, Cyberpunk is a new project of CD Projekt RED - The game is a continuation of the board game "Cyberpunk 2020", the action takes place 57 years later in the large city of Knight City, located in the state of California.

  • World premiere

    World premiere of the game took place on June 11, 2018 at E3, where an official trailer was unveiled and a demo was shown at a private press event.

  • Announcement

    In mid-2012, CD Projekt announced the development of a new game called Cyberpunk 2077.

  • Sales

    Invites users to hack a safe that is protected by an enemy team for a couple of minutes.

  • Showdown

    Similar to Battle Royale, where players in the amount of 10 people are dumped on the battlefield, which is slowly decreasing in size.

  • Game Place

    The game takes place in an urban area, and 3v3 teams will have to fight in real time.

  • Heroes

    At the moment, there are 22 heroes, each of them has his own weapon and ability.
